Company Guardrail

The company maintains a minimum margin floor to ensure variable compensation is funded by real profitability. If the rolling company margin drops below the guardrail threshold, variable compensation pauses — base salary and project bonuses are unaffected. This protects both you and the business during low-margin periods.
