Monthly Payroll Entries

Enter each month's GP Received (net of refunds) and Event GP Produced. The system auto-calculates commission using the employee's plan bands and monthly payout rules — you don't enter the $ paid, it's computed for you. Rows you've filled appear with a 🔒 and lock that month on the employee dashboard (read-only for the employee). Notes are a free-text audit trail.
