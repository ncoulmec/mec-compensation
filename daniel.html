Company Guardrail

The company maintains a minimum margin floor to ensure variable compensation is funded by real profitability. If the rolling company margin drops below the guardrail threshold, variable compensation pauses — base salary is unaffected. This protects both you and the business during low-margin periods.

Monthly Payout ?

Each month's GP Received runs through the monthly bands (annual ÷ 12). At the end of each quarter the third month is capped so the quarter total matches quarterly bands on quarterly GP. Month 12 also capped against annual entitlement. If you're ever overpaid, the cap sets that month's commission to $0 — no deduction from your paycheck. Each quarter reconciles on its own.
